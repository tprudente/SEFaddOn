SEF Boletins de Alojamento

About SEF Boletins de Alojamento Add-on

SEF Boletins de Alojamento is a powerful Google Sheets Add-on designed to streamline the process of sending Boletins de Alojamento (Accommodation Bulletins) to SEF (Serviço de Estrangeiros e Fronteiras) in Portugal.

How It Works

Once you install the Add-on, follow these simple steps to start automating the process:

  • Download a Google Forms template to input your accommodation data. You can access and download the template from here.
  • Install the SEF Boletins de Alojamento Add-on in your Google Sheets document.
  • The Add-on will process the data in your sheet and send the required information to SEF via Webservice.
  • If an error occurs during submission, the Add-on will automatically send you an email notification to keep you informed.

Why Choose SEF Boletins de Alojamento?

  • Efficient: Automates the tedious process of submitting accommodation reports to SEF, saving you time and reducing human error.
  • Secure: Your data is processed directly from your Google Sheet, and no private information is stored or shared. The Add-on only accesses your email to notify you if an error occurs.
  • Simple to Use: Easily integrate the Add-on into your Google Sheets document with just a few clicks.

Whether you're managing multiple accommodation records or looking for an easy way to stay compliant with SEF regulations, SEF Boletins de Alojamento is the ideal solution to simplify your workflow.
