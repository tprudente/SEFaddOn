Welcome to SEF Boletins de Alojamento

This Google Add-On helps you automate the submission of Boletins de Alojamento to SEF using your Google Sheets.

How It Works

To get started, follow the instructions below:

  1. Download a Google Forms template to input your accommodation data. You can access and download the template from here.
  2. After making a copy, you will become the owner of the form. You can then share the form link with your clients to collect their information.
  3. Once you start getting responses, navigate to the google sheets Answers and install the Add-on
  4. Click on SEF Definições -> Configurações and fill in your personal information
  5. Important: Do not modify the structure of the form as it may affect the functionality of the add-on.
  6. Note: Upon registration on SIBA website, if you did not choose the send method Webservice, you need to contact them and request to change it. You can find their contacts here

Why Use This Add-On?

  • Efficient: Automates the tedious process of submitting accommodation reports to SEF, saving you time and reducing human error.
  • Secure: Your data is processed directly from your Google Sheet, and no private information is stored or shared. The Add-on only accesses your email to notify you if an error occurs.
  • Simple to Use: Easily integrate the Add-on into your Google Sheets document with just a few clicks.
