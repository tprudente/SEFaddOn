Introduction

At SEF Boletins de Alojamento, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle and protect the data that you provide through our Google Sheets Add-on.

Data Access

Our Add-on does not collect or store any personal information beyond what is required for the Add-on’s functionality:

  • We use your Google email address to notify you if an error occurs during the data submission process.
  • We need access to your Google Sheets to open and edit the spreadsheet which contain the forms answers.

How We Use Your Data

  • Your email address is only used for error notifications related to the Add-on. We do not share, store, or process any other personal data.
  • Your spreadsheets are accessed solely for the purpose of retrieving and transmitting guest information to the foreign office via a web service. We do not share, store, or process this information in any other manner.

Security

Your data is processed securely by the Add-on, and no personal data is stored by us. The Add-on interacts directly with Google Sheets and uses secure web service calls to submit the Boletins de Alojamento to SEF.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices. We encourage you to check this page regularly for any updates.

Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to contact us.
