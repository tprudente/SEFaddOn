Privacy Policy

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how SEF Boletins de Alojamento ("the Add-on") handles your information when you use it in conjunction with Google Sheets. By using the Add-on, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

How the Add-on Works

The Add-on is designed to assist users in automating the process of sending Boletins de Alojamento via Webservice to SEF (Serviço de Estrangeiros e Fronteiras) based on data provided in their Google Sheets documents.

  • Runs automatically (daily or on-demand) as configured by the user.
  • Processes data within the user’s Google Sheet to prepare and send the required information to SEF.
  • Sends an email notification to the user in case an error occurs during processing.

Information We Access

The Add-on uses specific Google API scopes to perform its functions:

Non-Sensitive Scopes

  • Access to your Google Drive files: The Add-on accesses only the specific Google Sheets document where it is installed to read and process the data required to send Boletins de Alojamento.
  • View your primary email address: The Add-on uses your email address to identify you as the user.

Sensitive Scopes

  • Send email on your behalf: The Add-on sends emails to your email address only when an error occurs during processing. These emails include information about the error to assist in resolving the issue.

Data Handling

  • No Private Data is Received or Stored: The Add-on does not collect, store, or transmit private data to the Add-on owner or any third party.
  • On-the-Fly Processing: All processing is done locally within the user's Google Sheet and the SEF Webservice. The Add-on does not retain or manage any user data.
  • Email Notifications: Emails are sent only to the user’s primary Google Account email address in case of an error, ensuring no third-party data sharing occurs.

User Responsibilities

As the user, you are responsible for:

  • Ensuring the accuracy of the data entered into the Google Sheet.
  • Managing access permissions to the Google Sheet where the Add-on is installed.
  • Complying with local regulations regarding the handling of Boletins de Alojamento.

Data Security

The Add-on leverages Google’s secure API environment, ensuring that:

  • Data transmission occurs over secure connections (HTTPS).
  • Access to your Google Sheets and email is restricted to the specific permissions granted by you when installing the Add-on.

Third-Party Sharing

The Add-on does not share or sell your information to any third party. Data processed by the Add-on is sent directly to SEF's Webservice as configured by the user.
